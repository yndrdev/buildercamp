## Decisions
- **Domain-only auth**: No magic links, no OTP, no Supabase Auth. Just domain matching + cookie. Simplest possible auth.
- **Cookie session**: `bc_session` cookie with base64 JSON (email, clientSlug, clientId). 30-day expiry.
- **Standard tracks for all clients**: Leadership, Business Ops, Technology with 5 questions each. Auto-created on new client.
- **Claude Haiku 4.5 for everything**: Chat turns, greetings, analysis. Fast and cheap.
- **No service role key needed for auth**: Since we dropped magic links, auth works with just the anon key + domain check.
